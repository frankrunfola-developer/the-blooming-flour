Hi! I’m Kristina and I love to bake!

By day, I work as a Financial Analyst for a bank. When I first began my job at the bank, I felt that my naturally introverted personality would make it hard for me to make friends at work. So, I needed to come up with a way to make meeting people less ‘awkward’ for me. I called my mom and asked for her amazing chocolate chip cookie recipe (thanks mom!). I whipped up a batch, took them to work the next day, and introduced myself to as many people as I could. Not only was I met with kind and smiling faces all around, I realized that cookies brought joy to people. There’s just something about a fresh, delicious treat that warms the heart and makes people happy! I became hooked on this feeling of bringing people joy. Soon I was also making cupcakes, layer cakes, scones and muffins! While many people said I should sell my baked goods, I was content with just being a hobby baker for the time being.... As life would have it, I grew more adventurous in my baking and started to experiment with flavors and honing my craft in creative, new designs!

Even though my roots are in Buffalo, I recently spent 3 years in Costa Rica. I had the time of my life, met amazing people that have become family and forever friends, but my heart always knew I would end up back in Buffalo. Now that I’m back, I knew I wanted to take my baking to a new level and The Blooming Flour was born.

When I’m not baking, I love outdoor activities, especially hiking and skiing. The Adirondacks is my favorite place in NYS. I’m learning how to golf and it’s one of the most difficult things I’ve done to date. I love chocolate, wine, bakeries, coffee and checking out coffee shops!
